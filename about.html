about us:

    love u so matcha was created in 2017 by a group of matcha-loving girls who all worked together on making a happy & fun coffee shop environment. known around school for making some of the best drinks and muffins known to this earth, the girls started small by setting up stands every so often and selling their yummy treats. with drink stand business booming, the girls decided to pursue their dreams and create their own matcha & coffee shop, love u so matcha. here we are, 5 years later, and we couldn't be more happy to put smiles on people's faces when they experience that first-sip-feeling. :)
